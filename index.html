I’m PoYu Tseng — I do research in cryptography, especially zero-knowledge proofs (zk) and fully homomorphic encryption (FHE). I care about making advanced primitives usable in real systems: better constructions, concrete security, and implementations that don’t collapse under real-world constraints.
